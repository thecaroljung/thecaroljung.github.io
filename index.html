BEING

Creative Muse Juke, aka Carol M. Jung, is a designer based in Los Angeles.
She loves music and she hopes to open a music lounge tea bar mix some day in the near future. She envisions it as a safe space for musicians, poets, and artists to showcase their work.
Because of this, her work focuses on album art engraved on wood as well as music lyric typography design. She is also a big Pokemon and Marvel fan so makes pins to the side for fun.
Last but not least, she enjoys long walks on the beach.
